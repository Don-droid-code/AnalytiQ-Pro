# 📘 STRATEGIC MEMORANDUM – ANALYTIQ PRO (V.1.1)

**Date:** March 25, 2026  
**Status:** Design & Architecture  
**Expertise:** Business Data Analytics & Software Engineering

---

## 1. VISION & POSITIONING

- **Product:** A unified platform merging data cleaning (DataPrep) and financial evaluation (FinAnalyst).

- **The "AnalytiQ" Edge:** Unlike "black‑box" solutions (such as Julius AI), AnalytiQ Pro focuses on **total transparency**. Every data transformation generates visible, editable, and educational SQL/Python code.

- **Business Objective:** Democratize high‑precision analysis for SMEs (low‑cost / high‑volume model) in alignment with the **"Morocco 2030"** digitalization vision.

---

## 2. TECHNICAL & FUNCTIONAL PILLARS

### A. “Smart Cleaning” (Human‑AI Collaboration)

- **Moving Beyond “Auto‑Everything”:** Data cleaning is no longer passive; it is validated by the user.
- **Contextual Alert System:** Instead of blindly removing outliers, the AI queries the user regarding business reality (e.g., “Is this negative value a refund or an entry error?”).
- **Human‑Centric Design:** Cleaning becomes an audit phase where the analyst validates choices based on their specific business knowledge.

### B. Intelligence Engine (Gemini API)

- **Role:** The Gemini 1.5 Pro API acts as the architect. It interprets data, suggests cleaning steps, and writes Python/SQL code on the fly.
- **Cost Management:** Leveraging the Google AI Studio “Free Tier” for MVP development (0 MAD initial operational cost).

### C. Valuation Framework (Business Expertise)

- **The Core:** Powered by your 6‑dimension proprietary matrix (Financial, Market, Operations, Intangibles, Human Capital, Risk/ESG).
- **The Output:** An attractive interactive Dashboard that transforms Excel‑based scoring into narrative and visual reports for decision‑makers.

---

## 3. IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP (STEP‑BY‑STEP)

| Step | Focus | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **1. Ingestion** | Multi‑source | CSV/Excel upload. AI immediately identifies the business context. |
| **2. Audit & Cleaning** | Dialogue | Anomalies are presented as business questions for user validation. |
| **3. Processing** | Transparency | Real‑time display of Python/SQL code. The user “sees” the engine working. |
| **4. Valuation** | Insights | Application of the backend matrix logic to generate 1‑10 scores per department. |
| **5. Storytelling** | Dashboard | Generation of the final report (PDF/Interactive) with strategic recommendations. |
